Services

There are many convinient services here at Wymondham Allotments, Icluding:

  • Water
  • 2 Car Parks
  • Woodchip, rubble and manure piles
  • Picnic area by the river
  • Pallets

Apply

Each plot is let by the council, so to register your interest and get an application form, call or visit the Wymondham Town Council Offices, or alternatively download the application form here and drop it off at the office.

Wymondham Allotment Association Membership

It is possible to pick up an application form from the Wymondham Town Council Offices, and for a small fee, you will recieve a card giving you discount at Wymondham Garden Centre, Myhills, and John Does Wymondham
