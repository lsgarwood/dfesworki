About Wymondham Allotment Association

Plots

Each plot is let by council, and there are a total of 100 plots. Varying in size, and spread across an area of 6 acres.

Each plot grows a variery of plants. We grow veg and cut flowers.

Events

We regularly hold bacon butty and coffee mornings, and even hold an open day, where we sell fresh produce, and you can have a nosy round all of the wonderful plots.
